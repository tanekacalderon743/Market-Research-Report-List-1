<p><ul><li>Ionic Asphalt Emulsifiers</li><li>Non-ionic Asphalt Emulsifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The asphalt emulsifiers market is segmented into ionic and non-ionic types. Ionic asphalt emulsifiers, including cationic and anionic variants, are widely used for their strong adhesion and compatibility with aggregates, making them ideal for road construction and maintenance. Non-ionic asphalt emulsifiers, on the other hand, offer stability in diverse conditions and are less sensitive to pH changes, making them suitable for specialized applications like chip sealing and micro-surfacing. Both types enhance asphalt performance by improving emulsion stability and workability.</p></p>
