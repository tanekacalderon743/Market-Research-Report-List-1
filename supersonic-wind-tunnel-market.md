<p><p>A supersonic wind tunnel is a specialized facility used to simulate and study the aerodynamic properties of objects traveling at speeds exceeding the speed of sound (Mach 1). It is a critical tool in aerospace, defense, and automotive industries for testing aircraft, missiles, spacecraft, and other high-speed vehicles. Emerging trends include the development of compact, portable wind tunnels for smaller-scale testing and the adoption of sustainable practices to reduce energy consumption in tunnel operations.
