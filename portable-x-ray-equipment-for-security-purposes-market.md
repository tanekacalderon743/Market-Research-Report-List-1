<p><p>The portable X-ray equipment market for security purposes is categorized into two main types: Computed Radiography (CR) and Digital Radiography (DR). CR equipment uses traditional film processing techniques combined with digital image capture, making it versatile and cost-effective for various security applications. In contrast, DR equipment employs digital detectors that provide immediate imaging results and superior image quality, offering enhanced detection capabilities. Both types are essential in security settings like airports, borders, and critical infrastructure for effective threat assessment.</p></p>
