<p><p>The fluorogenic substrates market includes various types such as Fluorescein, MU, TFMU, Rhodamine, and others. Fluorescein substrates emit green fluorescence upon enzymatic cleavage, widely used in diagnostics and research. MU (4-Methylumbelliferyl) substrates release a blue fluorescent product, ideal for enzyme activity assays. TFMU (Trifluoromethylumbelliferyl) substrates offer enhanced sensitivity and stability, suitable for high-throughput screening. Rhodamine substrates produce red fluorescence, preferred for imaging and cellular studies. Other substrates cater to niche applications, providing diverse fluorescence properties for specialized research and diagnostic needs.</p></p>
