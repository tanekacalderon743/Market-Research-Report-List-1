<p><p>The Free-oil Scroll Vacuum Pump market is characterized by two main types: single-sided scroll and double-sided scroll. Single-sided scroll pumps utilize one scroll moving within a fixed scroll to create a vacuum, making them compact and efficient for smaller applications. In contrast, double-sided scroll pumps employ two scrolls to enhance performance, offering higher pumping speeds and greater capacity for larger systems. Both types are oil-free, providing clean vacuum solutions for various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food processing, and electronics.</p></p>
