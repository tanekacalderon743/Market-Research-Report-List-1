<p><p>The Digital Textile Inks market comprises various types tailored for specific fabric applications. Dispersed inks are ideal for synthetic fabrics, offering vibrant colors through a dye-sublimation process. Reactive inks bond with fibers, making them suitable for natural textiles like cotton, producing bright and durable designs. Acid inks are used for protein fibers such as silk and wool, delivering rich hues. Pigment inks, versatile for different substrates, sit on fabric surfaces, providing excellent durability and wash fastness.</p></p>
