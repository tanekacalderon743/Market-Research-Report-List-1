<p><strong>The Drafting Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One Side Matte</li><li>Two Side Matte</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drafting film comes in two primary market types: one side matte and two side matte. One side matte film features a matte finish on one side, providing a non-reflective surface ideal for drawing and drafting, while the other side is glossy, enhancing image clarity for presentations. Conversely, two side matte film is matte on both sides, offering versatility for various applications without glare, making it suitable for detailed work and easy note-taking. Both types cater to different user preferences and project needs.</p></p>
