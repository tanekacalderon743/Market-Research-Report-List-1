<p><p>The portable spectrum analyzer market comprises two main types: Frequency Sweep Type and Real-Time Spectrum Analyzers. Frequency Sweep Type analyzers scan through specific frequency ranges to identify signals over time, allowing for effective monitoring of frequency usage. In contrast, Real-Time Spectrum Analyzers capture and display signals as they occur, providing immediate data on transient events and enabling detailed analysis of complex waveforms. Both types are essential for applications in telecommunications, broadcasting, and electronic testing.</p></p>
