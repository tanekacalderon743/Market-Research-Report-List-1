The 6 – 10 mm range offers enhanced durability, making it suitable for construction and industrial uses. Sheets above 12 mm provide strong structural support, commonly used in heavy-duty applications like pallets and protective coatings. Each thickness serves distinct market needs based on strength and application.</p></p>
