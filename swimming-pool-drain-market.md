<p><p>The swimming pool drain market includes various types such as gravity drainage, multiple main drains, and others. Gravity drainage relies on natural water flow to remove water, often used in simple setups. Multiple main drains enhance safety and efficiency by distributing suction across several points, reducing the risk of entrapment. Other systems may include specialized designs like vacuum-assisted drains or hybrid models, catering to specific pool requirements. Each type offers unique benefits, balancing safety, efficiency, and installation complexity.</p></p>
