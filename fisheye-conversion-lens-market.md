<p><p>The fisheye conversion lens market is segmented into three main types: full-frame fisheye, circular fisheye, and others. Full-frame fisheye lenses provide a wide field of view that covers the entire image frame, creating a dramatic distortion effect ideal for landscapes and architecture. Circular fisheye lenses capture a 180-degree view, resulting in a circular image within the frame, often used for artistic effects. The "others" category includes specialty fisheye lenses that may cater to specific photography needs or unique applications.</p></p>
