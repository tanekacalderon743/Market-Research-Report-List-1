<p><p>The automotive paint guns market includes several types based on spray technology. High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP) guns use high airflow combined with low pressure to minimize paint overspray and maximize material transfer efficiency, making them environmentally friendly. Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP) guns operate at lower air volumes but maintain effective spray patterns, requiring less air compressor power. Other types may include airless spray guns, electrostatic paint guns, and traditional conventional spray guns, catering to various application needs and preferences in automotive painting.</p></p>
