<p><ul><li>EPDM (Synthetic Rubber) Cables</li><li>Polypropylene (Plastic) Cables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrical Submersible Pump (ESP) cable market offers two primary types: EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) cables and Polypropylene cables. EPDM cables are synthetic rubber options known for their high flexibility, resistance to moisture, and durability under extreme conditions, making them ideal for deep well applications. On the other hand, Polypropylene cables, made from plastic, are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and often used in less demanding environments. Both types play essential roles in ensuring efficient and reliable power supply to submersible pumps.</p></p>
