<p><p>The boat deck hatches market can be divided into two main types: watertight and non-watertight hatches. Watertight hatches are designed to prevent water ingress, ensuring safety and integrity in marine environments, making them essential for vessels operating in rough seas or adverse weather. Non-watertight hatches, on the other hand, serve applications where water resistance is less critical, allowing for easier access and ventilation in less demanding conditions. Both types cater to specific operational needs within the boating industry.</p></p>
