<p><p>The Portland Pozzolanic Cement market is segmented into two main types: Natural Pozzolana and Artificial Pozzolana. Natural Pozzolana is sourced from volcanic ash or natural deposits, known for its durability and sustainability in construction. In contrast, Artificial Pozzolana is manufactured from industrial by-products like fly ash, slag, or silica fume, providing specific performance enhancements and cost benefits. Both types enhance the properties of cement, improving strength and resistance to environmental degradation, making them vital in various construction applications.</p></p>
