<p><ul><li>Two-Layer Rigid-Flex Circuit</li><li>Multi-Layer Rigid-Flex Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rigid flex circuit board market is primarily divided into two types: two-layer rigid-flex circuits and multi-layer rigid-flex circuits. Two-layer rigid-flex circuits consist of one rigid layer and one flexible layer, offering a balance of affordability and versatility for simpler applications. In contrast, multi-layer rigid-flex circuits incorporate multiple layers of both rigid and flexible materials, providing enhanced functionality, density, and interconnectivity, making them ideal for complex applications in sectors such as aerospace, medical, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
