<p><p>The Rail Vehicle Sanitation System market features two primary types: Vacuum Toilet Systems and Pressured Water Flushing Toilet Systems. Vacuum Toilet Systems use a low-water, suction-based mechanism, efficiently removing waste with minimal water usage, making them eco-friendly and space-saving. In contrast, Pressured Water Flushing Toilet Systems utilize pressurized water to facilitate waste removal, providing a more traditional flushing experience. Both systems aim to enhance sanitation, comfort, and sustainability in rail vehicles, catering to the growing demand for modern travel infrastructure.</p></p>
