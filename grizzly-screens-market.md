<p><p>The grizzly screens market features two primary types: circular motion and linear motion. Circular motion screens utilize a rotary movement to separate materials, providing efficient stratification and high capacities, making them ideal for primary screening. Linear motion screens operate in a straight-line motion, delivering precise separation and better material control, suitable for secondary screening applications. Each type caters to specific material handling needs, enhancing productivity in industries like mining, aggregates, and recycling through tailored separation techniques.</p></p>
