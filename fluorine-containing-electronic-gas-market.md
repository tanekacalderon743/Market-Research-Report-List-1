<p><p>The fluorine-containing electronic gas market includes various types, each serving specific applications in the semiconductor and electronics industries. CF4 (carbon tetrafluoride) is used in plasma etching and cleaning processes, while C2F6 (hexafluoroethane) is utilized in etching silicon and silicon dioxide. C3F8 (octafluoropropane) is employed for etching and cleaning, and C4F8 (perfluorocyclobutane) is favored for its low global warming potential. CHF3 (fluoroform) is important in photolithography, while SF6 (sulfur hexafluoride) is used for insulation. NF3 (nitrogen trifluoride) acts as a cleaning agent. Other gases complement these applications.</p></p>
