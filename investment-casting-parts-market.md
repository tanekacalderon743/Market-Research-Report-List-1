<p><strong>The Investment Casting Parts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sodium Silicate Process</li><li>Tetraethyl Orthosilicate/ Silica Sol Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The investment casting parts market can be segmented into two primary processes: the Sodium Silicate Process and the Tetraethyl Orthosilicate/Silica Sol Process. The Sodium Silicate Process uses sodium silicate as a binder, offering cost-effectiveness and simpler handling, making it suitable for large-scale production. In contrast, the Tetraethyl Orthosilicate/Silica Sol Process utilizes a silica sol binder, providing superior surface finish and dimensional accuracy, catering to industries requiring high-precision components, such as aerospace and medical applications.</p></p>
