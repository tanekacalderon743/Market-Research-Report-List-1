<p><p>The water-based silica sol market is categorized into three main types: alkaline, acidic, and others. Alkaline silica sol is characterized by a higher pH, promoting stability and enhancing adhesion in various applications, including coatings and adhesives. Acidic silica sol, with a lower pH, is often favored for its superior dispersion and reactivity, making it suitable for electronics and ceramics. The "others" category includes specialized formulations tailored for unique industrial needs, such as enhanced thermal resistance or specific viscosity requirements.</p></p>
