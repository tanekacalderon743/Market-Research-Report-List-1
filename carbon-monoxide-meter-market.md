<p><p>The carbon monoxide meter market consists of three main types: biomimetic, electrochemical, and metal oxide semiconductor sensors. Biomimetic sensors mimic biological processes to detect CO levels accurately and can be highly sensitive. Electrochemical sensors rely on chemical reactions to monitor carbon monoxide concentrations, offering high specificity and reliability. Metal oxide semiconductor sensors utilize changes in electrical conductivity to detect CO, providing fast responses and durability. Each type serves varied applications in residential, industrial, and environmental monitoring contexts.</p></p>
