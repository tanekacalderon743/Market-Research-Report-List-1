<p><p>The glucan market is segmented into soluble and insoluble types based on their properties and applications. Soluble glucans, like beta-glucans, dissolve in water, forming gel-like substances that support immune health, cholesterol management, and blood sugar regulation. They are widely used in dietary supplements, functional foods, and pharmaceuticals. Insoluble glucans, such as cellulose, do not dissolve in water and primarily aid digestion by promoting gut health and regularity. They are commonly found in fiber-rich foods, animal feed, and industrial applications like biodegradable materials. Both types cater to diverse health and industrial needs.</p></p>
