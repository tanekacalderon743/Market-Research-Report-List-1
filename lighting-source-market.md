<p><p>The lighting source market is diverse, encompassing several key types. HID (High-Intensity Discharge) lamps provide bright illumination, often used in industrial applications. HPS (High-Pressure Sodium) lamps are known for their warm yellow light, frequently used in street lighting. Induction lighting offers high efficiency and long life, typically used in large spaces like warehouses. Each type has unique advantages suited for various applications.</p></p>
