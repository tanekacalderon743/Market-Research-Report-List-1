<p><strong>The Electronic Tongue Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potentiometric Type</li><li>Amperometric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic tongue market is primarily segmented into potentiometric and amperometric types. Potentiometric electronic tongues utilize ion-selective electrodes to measure the potential difference and determine the concentration of ions in solutions. They are widely used in quality control and food analysis. Amperometric electronic tongues, on the other hand, measure current changes in response to electrochemical reactions, providing insights into substance concentrations. Both types serve distinct applications in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and environmental monitoring by enabling rapid and accurate analysis.</p></p>
