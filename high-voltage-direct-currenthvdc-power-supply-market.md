<p><p>The High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC) power supply market is segmented into two main types: Positive Polarity HVDC Power Supply and Negative Polarity HVDC Power Supply. Positive Polarity HVDC systems transmit power using a positive voltage reference, offering advantages in reducing losses over long distances and improving grid stability. Conversely, Negative Polarity HVDC systems employ a negative voltage reference, effectively managing power flow and voltage stability. Both types are crucial for enhancing the efficiency and reliability of modern power transmission networks.</p></p>
