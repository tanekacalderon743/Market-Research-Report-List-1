<p><strong>The Retread Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Cure Process</li><li>Mold Cure Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Retread Tires Market includes two primary processes: Pre Cure and Mold Cure. In the Pre Cure Process, a pre-vulcanized tread is bonded to a prepared tire casing using a curing chamber, offering flexibility and faster production. The Mold Cure Process involves applying raw rubber tread to the casing and curing it in a mold, ensuring uniformity and durability. Both methods extend tire life, reduce costs, and promote sustainability by reusing tire casings, catering to different industry needs.</p></p>
