<p><ul><li>Moisture Cured Polysulfide Construction Sealant</li><li>Heat Cured Polysulfide Construction Sealant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polysulfide construction sealant market is segmented into two primary types: moisture-cured and heat-cured polysulfide sealants. Moisture-cured polysulfide sealants react with ambient moisture, providing excellent adhesion and resistance to environmental factors, making them ideal for exterior applications. In contrast, heat-cured polysulfide sealants require elevated temperatures to cure, yielding superior mechanical properties and chemical resistance, often utilized in demanding industrial settings. Both types are vital for ensuring durable, flexible, and weather-resistant sealing solutions in construction projects.</p></p>
