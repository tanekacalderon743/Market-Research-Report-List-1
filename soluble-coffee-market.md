<p><strong>The Soluble Coffee Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Drying</li><li>Freeze Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soluble coffee market primarily features two processing methods: spray drying and freeze drying. Spray drying involves atomizing a coffee extract into a hot air chamber, rapidly evaporating moisture and producing fine coffee powder. This method is cost-effective and results in a strong flavor. Conversely, freeze drying preserves more aroma and flavor by freezing the coffee extract and then removing moisture under vacuum. Both methods cater to differing consumer preferences for taste, texture, and convenience in soluble coffee products.</p></p>
