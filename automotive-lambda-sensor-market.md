<p><p>The Automotive Lambda Sensor market includes two main types: Titanium Oxide and Zirconia. Titanium Oxide sensors use changes in electrical resistance to measure oxygen levels in exhaust gases, offering durability and faster response times. Zirconia sensors, more common, generate a voltage difference based on oxygen concentration, providing high accuracy and reliability. Both types are crucial for optimizing fuel efficiency and reducing emissions in modern vehicles, with Zirconia dominating due to its widespread use and proven performance in emission control systems.</p></p>
