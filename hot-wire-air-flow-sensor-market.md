<p><p>The hot wire air flow sensor market is primarily divided into two types: electronic and mechanical. Electronic type sensors utilize a thin wire heated by an electric current, measuring airflow based on the temperature change as air passes over it. These sensors offer high precision and fast response times, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, mechanical type sensors rely on moving parts to detect airflow, often being less sensitive and slower but more robust, serving specific industrial applications where simplicity is valued.</p></p>
