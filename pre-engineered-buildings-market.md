<p><ul><li>Prefabricated Steel Structure Construction</li><li>Prefabricated Reinforced Concrete Construction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pre-engineered buildings market comprises two primary types: prefabricated steel structure construction and prefabricated reinforced concrete construction. Prefabricated steel structures utilize ready-made steel components, offering rapid assembly, high strength, and design flexibility, making them suitable for industrial, commercial, and agricultural applications. In contrast, prefabricated reinforced concrete construction involves the use of pre-cast concrete elements, providing durability and resistance to weather and fire, ideal for residential, institutional, and infrastructural projects. Both types emphasize efficiency, sustainability, and cost-effectiveness in modern construction.</p></p>
