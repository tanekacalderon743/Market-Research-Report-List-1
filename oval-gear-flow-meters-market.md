<p><p>Oval gear flow meters are precision instruments used for measuring the flow rate of various liquids, including viscous and corrosive fluids. They operate on the principle of positive displacement, where the flow causes two oval-shaped gears to rotate. As the gears turn, they trap a specific volume of liquid that is then discharged, allowing for accurate measurement of flow rate. Their robust design, coupled with the ability to handle a wide range of fluid viscosities, makes them suitable for diverse applications across industries such as chemical processing, oil and gas, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p><p>The Oval Gear Flow Meters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.1% during the forecast period.
