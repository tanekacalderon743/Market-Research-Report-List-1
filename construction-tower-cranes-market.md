<p><p>The construction tower cranes market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Self-Erecting Tower Cranes** are designed for quick setup and takedown, ideal for small to medium projects. </p><p>2. **Top-Slewing Tower Cranes** feature a horizontal jib that rotates around a fixed mast, suitable for large construction sites with high lifting capacities. </p><p>3. **Bottom-Slewing Tower Cranes** have the slewing mechanism at the base, offering better height flexibility and suitable for confined spaces. </p><p>4. **Other Market** includes specialized cranes like rail-mounted or mini cranes for unique construction needs.</p></p>
