<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Infrared Heat Wave Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared Detectors (SWIR)</li><li>Mid Wave Infrared Detectors (MWIR)</li><li>Long Wave Infrared Detectors (LWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared heat wave detectors market is categorized into three primary types based on the wavelength range they operate in. Short Wave Infrared Detectors (SWIR) are sensitive to wavelengths ranging from 0.9 to 1.7 micrometers, ideal for applications like surveillance and industrial monitoring. Mid Wave Infrared Detectors (MWIR), operating between 3 to 5 micrometers, are commonly used in thermal imaging and military applications. Long Wave Infrared Detectors (LWIR) function in the 8 to 14 micrometer range, primarily used for building security and environmental monitoring. Each type is tailored for specific applications, influencing their market dynamics.</p></p>
