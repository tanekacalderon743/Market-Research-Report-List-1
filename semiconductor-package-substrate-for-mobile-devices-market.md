<p><p>The semiconductor package substrate market for mobile devices includes several types. PBGA (Plastic Ball Grid Array) and CSP (Chip Scale Package) are traditional packaging methods, providing reliable performance. Other market types include emerging technologies catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
