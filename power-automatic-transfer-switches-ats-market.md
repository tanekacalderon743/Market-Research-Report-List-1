<p><ul><li>Open Transition</li><li>Closed Transition</li><li>Static Transfer Switch (STS)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS) are crucial for managing backup power systems. The market includes several types: Open Transition ATS switches power from a primary to a secondary source with a brief outage, ideal for non-critical loads. Closed Transition ATS allows for a seamless transfer, minimizing disruptions for sensitive applications. Static Transfer Switches (STS) use semiconductor devices for instantaneous switching, ensuring zero downtime. Other types include bypass switches and manual transfer systems, catering to diverse operational needs and reliability requirements.</p></p>
