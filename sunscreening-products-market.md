<p><ul><li>Chemical Sunscreen</li><li>Physical Sunscreen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sunscreen market is primarily divided into two types: chemical sunscreens and physical (or mineral) sunscreens. Chemical sunscreens absorb UV radiation through organic compounds, converting it into heat, and are often lightweight and easier to apply. In contrast, physical sunscreens contain active mineral ingredients, such as zinc oxide or titanium dioxide, that create a barrier on the skin's surface, reflecting UV rays. Both types offer protection, catering to different skin types and preferences, contributing to the diverse sunscreen market.</p></p>
