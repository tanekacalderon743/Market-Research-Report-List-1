<p><strong>The Polyimide Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seamless Tubing</li><li>Spiral Wound Tubing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyimide tube market comprises two main types: seamless tubing and spiral wound tubing. Seamless tubing is produced without seams or welds, offering superior strength, durability, and thermal stability, making it ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, spiral wound tubing consists of layers of polyimide material wound in a spiral manner, providing flexibility and resistance to extreme temperatures. Both types are used in various industries, including aerospace, electronics, and medical, due to their excellent mechanical and thermal properties.</p></p>
