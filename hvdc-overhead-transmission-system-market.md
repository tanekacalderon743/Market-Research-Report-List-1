<p><strong>The HVDC Overhead Transmission System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point-to-Point Transmission</li><li>Back-to-Back Station</li><li>Multi-Terminal System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVDC overhead transmission system market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Point-to-Point Transmission** connects two transmission endpoints, ideal for long-distance energy transfer, enhancing efficiency and minimizing losses. </p><p>2. **Back-to-Back Station** allows alternating current (AC) systems to connect directly, facilitating stability and control between grids. </p><p>3. **Multi-Terminal System** involves multiple connection points, enabling energy sharing among various locations, enhancing grid reliability, and supporting renewable integration. Each type plays a crucial role in modern energy distribution.</p></p>
