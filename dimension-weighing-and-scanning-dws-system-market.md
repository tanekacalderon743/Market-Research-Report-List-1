<p><p>Dimension Weighing and Scanning (DWS) systems are categorized into static and dynamic systems based on their operational methodology. Static systems involve measuring and weighing items while they are stationary, often used in less agile environments, ensuring accurate data collection for smaller volumes. In contrast, dynamic systems measure items in motion, making them suitable for high-throughput situations like logistics and shipping. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in inventory management, aiding businesses in optimizing space and reducing costs.</p></p>
