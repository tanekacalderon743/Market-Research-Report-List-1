<p><p>The Vehicle Foams Market encompasses various foam types utilized in automotive applications. Polyurethane foams offer excellent cushioning and insulation, making them ideal for seat and interior applications. Polyolefin foams are lightweight, durable, and often used for sound absorption. Styrenic foams provide excellent thermal resistance and are used in various components. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) foams are versatile and often used in interior trims. Phenolic foams are renowned for their fire resistance, while melamine foams are lightweight and offer sound-dampening properties.</p></p>
