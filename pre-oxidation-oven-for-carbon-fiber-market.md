<p><p>The pre-oxidation oven for carbon fiber processing includes three main types: Cross-Flow, Parallel-Flow, and Down-Flow oxidation ovens. Cross-Flow ovens utilize a design that promotes air circulation from opposite sides, enhancing uniform temperature distribution. Parallel-Flow ovens allow air to flow parallel to the fiber, optimizing thermal efficiency. Down-Flow ovens direct heated air downward over the material, ensuring consistent exposure to heat. Each type serves distinct operational needs and optimizes the pre-oxidation stage critical for producing high-quality carbon fibers.</p></p>
