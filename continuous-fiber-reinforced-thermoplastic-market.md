<p><p>Continuous Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (CFRT) is a composite material that combines thermoplastic polymers with continuous fibers, such as carbon, glass, or aramid, to create lightweight, high-strength, and durable components. CFRT offers excellent mechanical properties, corrosion resistance, and recyclability, making it ideal for industries like automotive, aerospace, construction, and consumer goods. Its ability to be molded into complex shapes and its cost-effectiveness compared to traditional materials have driven its adoption.</p><p>The Continuous Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period. The construction industry is also adopting CFRT for its durability and resistance to environmental factors.</p><p>Emerging trends include the development of bio-based thermoplastics and hybrid composites to enhance sustainability.
